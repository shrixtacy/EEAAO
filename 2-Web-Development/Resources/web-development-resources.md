## üöÄ **SUCCESS TIPS**

### **Learning Strategy**
- **80/20 Rule**: 20% learning, 80% building projects
- **Consistency**: 1-2 hours daily beats 10 hours once a week
- **Project-Based**: Always build something with what you learn
- **Community**: Join Discord servers, Reddit communities, local meetups

### **Budget-Friendly Tips**
- **Library Access**: Many libraries offer free access to LinkedIn Learning, Pluralsight
- **Student Discounts**: GitHub Student Pack includes many free resources
- **Free Trials**: Use free trials strategically for intensive learning periods
- **Open Source**: Contribute to open source projects for real-world experience

### **Time Management**
- **Morning Learning**: Study complex topics when your mind is fresh
- **Commute Practice**: Use mobile apps during commute
- **Weekend Projects**: Dedicate weekends to building projects
- **Pomodoro Technique**: 25-minute focused sessions with breaks

Remember: The best resource is the one you'll actually use consistently. Start with free resources, build projects, and invest in paid resources as your needs become clearer!
